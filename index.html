About The Studio
Est. 2024

More Than
A Gym

NT Lifestyle and Fitness Studio is Poonamallee's most advanced fitness destination. Our expansive facility spans over 5,000 sq. meters, equipped with 30+ international-grade machines and led by certified professionals who are passionate about your results.

Whether you're a beginner starting your journey or an athlete pushing limits, our studio provides the space, equipment, and expert guidance to help you reach your goals.

What We Offer

Our Services

01
Weight Training

Build strength and sculpt your physique with our full range of free weights, barbells, and plate-loaded machines from leading international brands.

02
Personal Training

One-on-one coaching with our certified trainers. Get a custom program tailored to your goals, body type, and schedule for maximum results.

03
Group Classes

High-energy group sessions including cardio circuits, core training, and functional fitness. Train harder, together, with expert instructors leading every class.

04
Cardio Zone

Dedicated cardio floor with treadmills, ellipticals, rowing machines, and bikes. Improve endurance and burn calories in a motivating environment.

05
Functional Fitness

Dedicated functional training zone with kettlebells, battle ropes, TRX suspension systems, and agility equipment for athletic performance.

06
Nutrition Guidance

Complementary nutrition advice to support your training. Our coaches help you fuel your body right for faster, sustainable results.
